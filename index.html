Where is RobotsConf 2015?

RobotsConf was replaced with the JSConf US Last Call this year. Due to an unfortunate medical issue with the organizers, the decision was made to have a RobotsConf like party during the JSConf US Last Call event.

We hope you keep RobotsConf and all the time together in your memories and thoughts and as Virginia Williams coined,
